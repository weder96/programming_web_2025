We don´t believe in staying the same.
We believe in getting better!

Individual therapy is for anyone feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or disconnected. Maybe everyday stress is taking a toll, or you’re facing something bigger, like trauma or a major life transition. Therapy helps when you’re feeling lost, unsure of yourself, or just not like your usual self.

If you’re struggling with emotions, stress, or making decisions, t herapy can give you the tools to feel more in control and at peace.

At Silicon Valley Therapy, we’ve got a team of caring, experienced therapists—both male and female—who are fully licensed and ready to support you. You can feel confident knowing your sessions are private and handled by professionals who’ve helped countless others through similar struggles.

Why Work With Silicon Valley Therapy ?

At Silicon Valley Therapy, we believe that life is about progress, not perfection.

When the demands on your time and energy are constant, the pressure to make it look like everything is ok can lead to burnout, stress, and a disconnection from your own needs.

The speed of life, coupled with the pressure to succeed, makes it easy to lose touch with what truly matters—leaving you feeling overwhelmed, hurting, and unfulfilled. Trying to “think” your way out of your pain is a short-term solution for a situation that requires deeper emotional understanding and support.

With over 28 years of combined experience, our team is dedicated to providing results-driven, client-centered, and compassionate care. We work with you to achieve a deeper understanding of yourself, identify and change patterns that lead to unwanted outcomes, and help you reach your goals in all areas of life.
